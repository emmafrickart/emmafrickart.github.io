About Me

Welcome to my art portfolio website!
My name is Emma Frick. I am currently a senior at Strath Haven High School. Art -- whether it be sketching, drawing, painting, creative writing, music, ceramics, etc -- has been a hobby of mine for as long as I can remember. While I am undecided whether or not I will have a future career in art, it will always be a very important outlet in my life. In high school I have been fortunate enough to further develop my creativity skills primarily in studio art. I have taken Intro to 2D Art, Advanced Painting and Printmaking, Fine Arts Portfolio, and currently, AP Studio Art. This website is a compilation of work that represents me as an artist.
Thank you for viewing!
